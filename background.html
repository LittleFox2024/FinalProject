Why is this important?

Everyday we see and care about the dogs, cats, and other pets all around us. They are important to us and are treated like children. But yet we keep buying them from breeders without caring about what it means to them, or to us.

Buying from a breeder may seem like no big deal, but there is actually a lot going on when we do. Breeders usually care about profit over the dogs themselves, and the dogs are often not treated well. Puppy mills are a specific example of this.
